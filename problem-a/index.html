Spider-Man

The fictional character I admire is Spider-Man. He is from the Marvel Universe and his first appearance is in August 1962. Spider-Man's real identity is Peter Parker. He is a high school student from Queens, New York. Below are three reasons why I admire Spider-Man.

  • Spider-Man can shoot webs from his wrists.
  • Spider-Man is not scared of spiders.
  • Spider-Man's costume is really cool.
